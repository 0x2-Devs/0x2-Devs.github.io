// who we are

We actually care about what we build.

We're two experienced software developers based in Adelaide, South Australia. Between us we've designed, built, and shipped software used by real teams and real businesses — with years of experience turning complex problems into things that are actually a pleasure to use.

We started 0x2 Devs because we got tired of software that exists just to exist. Apps that are clunky, overcomplicated, or just plain unpleasant to use. We think people deserve better — and we're doing something about it.

Our name is a bit of a nerdy in-joke — in the number system programmers use, 0x2 is just the number two. Two developers, one goal: quality.

// what drives us
  • Quality over quantity A few great apps beats a pile of forgettable ones, every time.
  • User-first thinking If it's frustrating to use, it doesn't matter how clever it is under the hood.
  • Privacy & trust We treat your data the way we'd want ours treated — with respect.
  • Craft in the details Fast, reliable, and thoughtfully built from the ground up.
